# Auto Lock-in

> *Because you deserve to make a sandwich in peace.*

---

## The Problem

You queue up for a game. Estimated wait: 4 minutes. So naturally you:

- Go to the kitchen to make a sandwich
- Stop to pee
- Answer a phone call from someone you've been ignoring for 3 days
- Come back to find you've been autofilled, dodged by accident, and lost 20 LP

Sound familiar? Yeah. We've all been there.

## The Solution

**Auto Lock-in** handles everything for you the moment the queue pops.

- Accepts the ready check — even if your hands are full
- Detects which role you got assigned (including autofill, second position, all of it)
- Bans your champion automatically
- Locks in your pick before someone else steals it
- Sets your runes and summoner spells
- Even sends a "gl hf" in chat so you look like a decent human being

You just set it up once, go live your life, and come back to a loaded game screen.

## Setup (No Python, just download and run)

1. Download `Auto Lock-in.exe` from the [latest release](https://github.com/bereanu99/LOL-AUTO-LOCK-IN/releases/tag/V1.0.0)
2. Run it
3. Open League of Legends
4. Go make that sandwich

> Your settings are saved automatically — you only need to set things up once.

---

## How to use it

The app has **5 slots**, one for each role. Fill in your champion, ban, runes, and spells for each role. When a game starts, it detects your assigned position and switches to the right slot automatically.

So whether you queued as mid and got autofilled support, or you're just too distracted to click — it's handled.

---

## The origin story

I built this while queuing up for League. Not because it was a great plan, but because the queue was taking forever and I had nothing better to do. Somewhere between ban phase and champion select the first version was working. Then I kept adding features every queue. Now it does basically everything except actually play the game for you (that's a future release).
